Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To reduce unemployment and empower low-income youth in Rwanda by providing job placement services, skill development programs, government initiative support, and entrepreneurship resources. We aim to enhance the country's GDP, improve living standards, and maximize the potential of young talents.

Problem Statement

According to the World Bank, 21.97% of young people aged from 18 to 24 in Kigali Rwanda are affected by a lack of job opportunities which is a slight decrease from 2022. The key challenges that they are faced with are limited job market access, lack of adequate experience, and skills mismatch between workforce and job demands. This not only affects individuals but also the economy in general and this problem is consistence due to factors such as an increase in population and economic fluctuations.
